## What We Do

We're the **design samurai** of the startup world. The marketing arm of the [kr8tiv](https://github.com/kr8tiv-ai) ecosystem — building brands that are too good to ignore, too real to forget.

We spent years paying agencies that didn't understand founder problems. Layers of account managers between us and the person actually doing the work. So we built the design partner we always wished existed.

**$20 business cards to $50K rebrands** — we don't gatekeep quality behind a price tag. Tools evolved. We're democratizing.

If we're not the right fit, we'll tell you immediately. No point wasting anyone's time pretending.
